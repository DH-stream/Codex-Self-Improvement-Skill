# Core invariants

Load this before modifying the self-improvement engine or its memory.

1. **User intent and correctness come first.**
2. **Fire-and-forget means automatic and quiet, not invisible risk.**
3. **Efficiency may reduce repetition, scope, and narration; never required evidence.**
4. **One global engine serves every repository.**
5. **Private user learning and public universal learning are separate storage domains.**
6. **Personal taste and private evidence never enter public branches, commits, issues, or PRs.**
7. **Project facts are context, not global learning.**
8. **Explicit user corrections outrank inference.**
9. **One qualified universal improvement may open a draft PR; batching is not required.**
10. **Universal contributions use dedicated branches from current upstream `main`.**
11. **Direct pushes to `main` and automatic merge are forbidden.**
12. **Nothing is deleted. Lower relevance or mark superseded and preserve evidence.**
13. **Original evidence is immutable; later assessments append.**
14. **Routine reflection must cost less than the waste it prevents.**
15. **No write means no notice; a real write produces one compact filename/PR notice.**
16. **Engine changes are tracked and pressure-tested; ordinary learning updates memory.**
17. **Never claim success without fresh verification appropriate to the task.**
